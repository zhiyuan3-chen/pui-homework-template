"Black Square" (1915)

Artworks

Created in 1915, “Black Square” stands as an iconic and revolutionary work in the realm of abstract art. It is a radical departure from representational art, and challenges the viewer to reconsider the very nature of art and perception.

Malevich referred to this work as the "zero point of painting," signaling a departure from the past and an invitation to explore the limitless potential of abstract expression.
