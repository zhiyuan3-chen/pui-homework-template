“Suprematist Composition: Airplane Flying” (1915)

Artworks

This artwork reflects the influence of modern technology on his artistic vision. In the composition, a geometrically abstracted airplane soars dynamically through the canvas, stripped of representational elements and reduced to essential geometric forms.

The use of angular lines and bold shapes conveys a sense of movement and velocity, echoing the dynamism of technological advancements in the early 20th century.
